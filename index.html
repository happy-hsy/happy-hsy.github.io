Haosen Yang

I'm a Ph.D. student at the People-Centred AI Institute (PAI) and the Centre for Vision, Speech, and Signal Processing (CVSSP) at the University of Surrey. I conduct research within the Universal Perception Lab (UP) under the supervision of Dr. Xiatian Zhu.

My research interests primarily lie in Human-Centric Spatial Intelligence. My goal is to assist humans in understanding, digitizing, and manipulating the physical world while seamlessly bridging reality with the metaverse to enhance everyday interactions.

Feel free to email me if you're interested in my work or exploring potential collaborations.
